# Low End Translation Reference

Kick and bass need separate jobs. One sound should own the deepest sub range, usually 45 Hz to 80 Hz, while the other carries punch or note definition higher up.

If kick and bass blur together, use sidechain compression or dynamic EQ instead of simply turning one down. Check the groove in mono because wide low frequencies often disappear or become unstable on small speakers.

For club masters, keep more low-end density but protect headroom with true-peak limiting. For streaming masters, use less limiter pressure and leave safer codec headroom.
