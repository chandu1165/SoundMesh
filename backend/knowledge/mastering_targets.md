# Mastering Targets Reference

Streaming masters should usually prioritize translation over maximum loudness. A practical target is around -14 LUFS integrated with a true peak near -1 dBTP.

Podcast and speech content often works around -16 LUFS stereo or -19 LUFS mono, with stronger noise control and more consistent vocal dynamics.

Club versions can be louder, often around -9 LUFS to -10 LUFS, but they need careful low-end control and should avoid harsh limiting that destroys transient punch.
